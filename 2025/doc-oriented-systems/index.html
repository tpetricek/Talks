Merging

\(\mathcal{M}_E(E_1, E_2) = E, E_1, E_2'\)

Edit reconciliation

Given \(e_1\) and \(e_2\), produce \(e_2', e_2'', \ldots\) that can be applied after \(e_1\)

  • Apply \(e_1\) to nodes newly added by \(e_2\)
